🚀 First-Time Setup

When you open the app, you'll see a Connect Supabase screen. This is expected — the app gracefully handles missing credentials.

Enter your VITE_SUPABASE_URL and VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY in the form, or create a .env.local file in the project root:

VITE_SUPABASE_URL=https://xxxxx.supabase.co

VITE_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY=eyJhbGciOi...

After connecting, the full menu app will load with your restaurant data.
